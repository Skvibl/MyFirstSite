Beer

2020 Pivnuha

Beer is a low-alcohol beverage produced by the alcoholic fermentation of malt wort (usually barley) using brewer's yeast, and the addition of hops for flavor and aroma. Depending on the recipe and additives, there are many different types of beer. According to Russian law, beer consists of malt, hops, and water, while the content of other ingredients (such as sugar) must not exceed 2%.

Calvados

2018Dom Andreya

Calvados is a spirit (fruit brandy) produced in the Normandy region of France by distilling apple and pear cider. After distillation, the drink is aged in oak barrels, imparting a rich apple aroma and complex flavor. The quality of Calvados is strictly regulated by French law, which defines the fruit varieties, production methods and aging time.

Vodka

2018Red and White

Vodka is a strong alcoholic drink, a colorless water-alcohol solution obtained by mixing rectified ethyl alcohol with prepared water. To achieve a neutral taste and aroma, the drink undergoes multiple distillations and filtration (often using activated carbon).
